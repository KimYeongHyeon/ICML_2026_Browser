# ICML 2026 Main Conference Materials Collection Plan

Main question: Which ICML 2026 main-conference accepted paper materials are publicly obtainable from official or first-party sources?

Subtopics:

1. Official accepted-list gate
   - Check ICML 2026 official pages and OpenReview for a public, enumerable main-conference accepted list.
2. Record enumeration
   - Extract official IDs, titles, authors, and paper/material URLs only for main-conference accepted papers.
3. Material download
   - Download paper PDFs and any public supplemental links from official ICML/OpenReview records without authentication or reviewer-only access.
4. Reporting
   - Write a JSONL manifest and concise README with discovered, downloaded, skipped, failed, and blocker counts.

Synthesis rule: If the accepted list is not publicly enumerable, stop short of inference and report the blocker clearly.
